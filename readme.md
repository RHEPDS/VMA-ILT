# **Virtualization Migration Assessment \- Technical Workshop ILT**

There is a significant market opportunity to support customers in transitioning from VMware-based infrastructure to OpenShift. This workshop is designed to equip technical sellers and consultants with the expertise to position and secure customer commitments for a Virtualization Migration Assessment (VMA).

## **At a Glance**

* **Goal**: Gain confidence in identifying, qualifying, and convincing customers to commit to a VMA.  
* **Primary Audience**: Technical Sales, PreSales, Business Consultants, and Delivery Consultants.  
* **Duration**: 3 Days.  
* **Key Focus**: Assessing migration risks, running assessments as a partner, and scoping Proof of Concepts for OpenShift Virtualization.

## **Pre-Requisites**

Participants should have the following foundational knowledge prior to attending:

* **OpenShift Knowledge**: DO180.  
* **OpenShift Virt**: Basic understanding of the technology.  
* **VMware**: General technical knowledge.

## **Workshop Objectives**

Upon completion of this workshop, participants will be able to:

* Position the technical and business value of the VMA.  
* Perform Technical Discovery and Risk Assessments.  
* Determine when to engage Ecosystem partners.  
* Handle competitive technical or business situations.  
* Present OpenShift Virt and lead hands-on customer workshops effectively.
